AVIORA MedTech partners with world-class manufacturing facilities certified to ISO 13485:2016, ensuring every surgical device meets the same engineering and quality benchmarks regardless of where it is used.

Manufacturing philosophy

The right partner makes a better device than any single facility can alone.

Rather than invest in a single facility, AVIORA MedTech identifies and partners with globally certified specialist manufacturers — facilities that have spent years refining one product category. The result is a device range where each product comes from the most appropriate expert, not the most convenient factory.

Standards

Seven pillars our manufacturing partners are held to.

Quality Systems

ISO 13485:2016 certified QMS with documented procedures for design, production, incoming and outgoing inspection.

Product Development

Design controls aligned to IEC 62366 (usability) and ISO 14971 (risk management), with documented design history files.

Engineering & Tolerances

Critical dimensions — staple height, jaw closure force, cartridge fit — are controlled to specification with in-process measurement, not only final inspection.

Validation

Process validation (IQ/OQ/PQ) is documented for all critical manufacturing steps. Validation records are available on request.

Sterilisation

Gamma and EO sterilisation with cycle validation and biological indicator testing per ISO 11135 / ISO 11137. SAL 10⁻⁶ maintained.

Regulatory Support

Manufacturing partners maintain CE technical files and, where applicable, FDA 510(k) clearances. AVIORA MedTech holds CDSCO import registration for all supplied product lines.

Partner Transparency

Where permitted by our supply agreements, we disclose manufacturing partner identity and certification details to hospital procurement on request.
