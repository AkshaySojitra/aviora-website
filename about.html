About AVIORA

Surgical devices built around clinical trust.

AVIORA MedTech is a registered importer and distributor of single-use surgical devices in India. We work with ISO 13485 certified manufacturing partners to supply hospitals and surgical centres with devices that meet exacting quality standards.

Our Vision

A standard of surgical precision that every Indian hospital can access.

We believe surgeons deserve devices that perform consistently — not just in private tertiary centres but in every operating theatre across India.

Our Mission

Source, qualify and supply — with complete traceability.

We identify manufacturing partners with documented quality systems, qualify each device against clinical specifications, and supply with full lot-level documentation from import to hospital delivery.
