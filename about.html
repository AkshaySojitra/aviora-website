About AVIORA

Single-use surgical devices for hospitals and surgical centres across India.

AVIORA MedTech is a CDSCO registered importer and distributor of single-use surgical devices. We source from ISO 13485 certified manufacturing partners and supply with full lot documentation and technical support.

What you receive

Products backed by documentation, not just packaging.

ISO 13485 certified manufacturing
All partners operate under a certified quality management system.
CE documentation on request
Technical files and Declaration of Conformity for applicable lines.
Full lot traceability
Every unit linked to its batch, sterilisation cycle and distribution record.
EO sterilised, SAL 10⁻⁶
Validated sterilisation with cycle records retained per lot.
Technical and clinical support
Application specialists for first cases, training and product queries.
Our position

Registered importer. Not a trader.

Every product we supply has been qualified against a defined specification, sourced from an audited manufacturer, and cleared through incoming inspection before it enters stock.

We do not list products we cannot support with documentation. If we cannot provide the CE file, ISO certificate or sterilisation record — we do not supply the product.
