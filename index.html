Who we are

A focused instrument maker, not a general-line distributor.

Aviora Medtech works on one category — endo surgery — so every design review, every incoming inspection and every mould change is made by people who only think about staplers, trocars and clip appliers. That focus shows up as consistent firing force, consistent jaw closure, and consistent lot-to-lot performance in theatre.
