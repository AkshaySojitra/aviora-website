Quality & Compliance

Every shipment backed by documented quality checkpoints.

Our quality framework covers supplier qualification, incoming inspection, sterility assurance and post-market surveillance — so every device that leaves our warehouse is traceable and compliant.

Quality process

Six checkpoints, every shipment.

Supplier Qualification

Manufacturing partners are audited against ISO 13485, CE certification and CDSCO eligibility before any order is raised.

Incoming Inspection

Every batch is sampled — packaging integrity, label compliance, lot documentation — before release to stock.

Lot Traceability

Every unit links to its manufacturing batch, sterilisation cycle and import record. Data retained for shelf-life plus five years.

Sterility Assurance

EO or gamma sterilisation with SAL 10⁻⁶. Cycle validation records available on request per lot.

Complaint Handling & CAPA

Every field complaint is logged, investigated and escalated through a formal CAPA process with root-cause feedback to the partner.

Continuous Improvement

Annual partner audits and post-market surveillance data drive ongoing improvement across sourcing and logistics.
