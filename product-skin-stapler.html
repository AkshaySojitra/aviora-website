Overview

Rapid, reliable wound closure.

The AVIORA Single Use Skin Stapler delivers consistent wide-crown stainless steel staples for fast closure of linear incisions. The matching remover is supplied in the same pack, allowing atraumatic staple removal at the appropriate time. Suitable across general, orthopaedic and trauma surgery.

Clinical Applications
General surgical wound closure
Orthopaedic incision closure
Trauma and emergency wound closure
Caesarean section skin closure
Any linear incision requiring skin stapling
Key Features

Fast closure, consistent results.
