Overview

Designed specifically for stapled hemorrhoidopexy.

The AVIORA hemorrhoidal stapler is designed for the Procedure for Prolapse and Haemorrhoids (PPH). It resects a circumferential band of prolapsed mucosal tissue and repositions the haemorrhoidal cushions above the dentate line in a single firing. The procedure is associated with significantly reduced post-operative pain compared to conventional excisional haemorrhoidectomy, as the staple line is placed in non-innervated tissue.

Indicated for Grade II, III and IV haemorrhoidal prolapse and circumferential mucosal prolapse.

Key Features

Design & engineering details.

33mm circular staple line
Circumferential resection above the dentate line — consistent staple formation in non-innervated tissue.
Reduced post-operative pain
Staple line placed proximal to the dentate line, below the pain-sensitive squamous epithelium.
Single-fire design
Factory-loaded, single-use. No reloading or reprocessing. Consistent anvil-to-cartridge gap on every use.
Ergonomic single-hand grip
Designed for controlled advancement and firing during the procedure.
