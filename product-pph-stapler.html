Overview

Designed specifically for hemorrhoidopexy.

The AVIORA Single Use Hemorrhoidal Stapler is engineered for the Procedure for Prolapse and Hemorrhoids (PPH). A 33mm circular staple line repositions prolapsed haemorrhoidal tissue above the dentate line, reducing pain, bleeding and recovery time compared to conventional excisional techniques.

Clinical Applications
Procedure for Prolapse and Hemorrhoids (PPH)
Grades II, III and IV hemorrhoidal prolapse
Circumferential mucosal prolapse resection
Rectal mucosal prolapse procedures
Key Features
