Overview

Designed for safe, efficient abdominal access in MIS.

The AVIORA Disposable Laparoscopic Bladeless Trocar is a syringe-shaped instrument used during laparoscopic surgery to puncture the abdominal wall, create working tunnels and maintain pneumoperitoneum throughout the procedure.

A patented bladeless olecranon design avoids the risk of injury to internal organs and eliminates the need for fascia suture, while an elastic sealing system and universal seal design accommodate instruments from 5mm to 12mm without a converter.

Clinical applications
Diagnostic and operative laparoscopy
Gastrointestinal laparoscopic surgery
Gynaecological and urological MIS
Bariatric surgery — extra-long variants available
Paediatric surgery — small head 5mm variant
Thyroid and thoracic minimally invasive access
Features & Benefits

Four design advantages that matter in theatre.

Based on the clinical features described by our manufacturing partner, each design element solves a specific problem surgeons face with conventional trocars.

Patented bladeless olecranon design

The bladeless entry system avoids the risk of injury to internal organs and eliminates the need for fascia suture. The design allows for safe expansion of the abdominal wall, reducing postoperative pain compared to bladed trocars.

✓No internal organ injury risk
✓Fascia suture not required
✓Reduced postoperative pain

Bivalved elastic sealing

A bivalved elastic sealing design ensures a secure, airtight seal, minimising the risk of gas leakage and maintaining pneumoperitoneum throughout the procedure — enhancing the efficiency and safety of laparoscopic surgery.

✓Secure, airtight pneumoperitoneum
✓Minimal gas leakage risk
✓Stable working environment

Universal seal design

Provides perfect wrapping around the instrument rod to prevent leakage. A three-layer structure with special materials ensures excellent gas-proof performance, accommodating instruments from 5mm to 12mm — no converter required.

✓5mm to 12mm compatibility
✓No converter needed
✓Three-layer gas-proof seal

Thread design

The threaded cannula design strengthens abdominal wall fixation without the need for suture fixation, ensuring stable operation access throughout the entire procedure. Available across the full size range from 5mm to 15mm.

✓Secure abdominal wall fixation
✓No suture fixation required
✓Stable port throughout procedure
